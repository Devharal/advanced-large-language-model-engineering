## How to Update
1. After finishing a module's readiness checklist, tick "Readiness Map".
2. After condensing all "Concepts to Learn" into `notes/`, tick "Topic Notes".
3. After implementing every "What to Implement" item, tick "Implementations".
4. After the Core Engineering Project's acceptance checklist is fully satisfied,
   tick "Core Project" and update Status to "Complete".
